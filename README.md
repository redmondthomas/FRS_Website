## Business Case: Farm Relief Services (FRS)
FRS (Farm Relief Services) is a farmer-owned co-operative organisation established in 1980 for the provision of skilled people to meet customer requirements.
Currently at 17 locations across the country, they have been providing the skills and services, which customers need to manage their farms efficiently, effectively and successfully for over 45 years.
They provide both specialist and general farm labour such as:
Slurry & Fertiliser Spreading, Topping, Hay & Silage Making, Moving & Herding Cattle for TB Testing/Dosing along with Tractor Driving and many other farm tasks.
Specialist Services include:
Milking, Hoof Care, Cow & Sheep Scanning, Freeze Branding, Dehorning, Farm Plastic Collection, Milk Recording, Weighing, Contract Fencing, DIY Fencing Sales and Training.
FRS are looking to create a modern, user-friendly website to act as a source of information about these services and serve as a booking platform as well.
