Our Farm Services

Extra manpower for daily tasks, seasonal peaks, or emergencies.

Calf Dehorning

Safe and efficient dehorning performed by trained personnel.

Pregnancy Scanning

Accurate and fast scanning services for herd management.

Animal Weighing

Portable livestock weighing for growth tracking and herd planning.

Fencing Services

Secure, durable fencing solutions for farms of all types.

Milk Recording

Reliable data collection for performance monitoring and herd improvement.

Farm Plastic Collection
