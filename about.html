About Us

Who We Are

FRS (Farm Relief Services) is a farmer-owned co-operative established in 1980 to provide skilled personnel to meet the needs of farm customers.

Our Objective

To provide the skills and services farmers need to manage their farms efficiently, effectively and successfully.

Our Commitment to Quality

  • FRS is committed to providing top-quality service to farmers.
  • FRS operators are selected and trained to the highest standards to meet farm requirements.
  • Applicants join the FRS contractor register only after successful evaluation and supervision.

Why Use FRS Farm Services?

Reputable Quality Service

Cost Effective

  • Book an operator only for the days required.
  • Operators work the hours requested by you at a flat rate per hour.
  • No hidden costs.
  • No advertising, recruitment or employee costs such as PAYE, PRSI, holidays, etc.
  • Fees paid to FRS are fully tax deductible.

General Farm Labour

  • Slurry & Fertiliser Spreading • Topping • Hay & Silage Making
  • Moving & Herding Cattle for TB Testing / Dosing • Tractor Driving
  • …and many other farm tasks.

How to use FRS Farm Services

  • Contact your local frs office and outline your requirements.
  • The co-ordinator selects the most suitable local operator to service your needs.
  • On completion of the work the operator completes a docket - a copy of which is left with you and a further copy is sent to the frs office.
  • A detailed Invoice is sent to you. All fees, including annual membership, are paid directly to your local frs office.
